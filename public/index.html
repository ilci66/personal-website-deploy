My Projects

I always try something new in my personal projects. Here are some of them:

Auction App

Used express, node.js, mongodb and react-redux, and for authentication used json web tokens.

Users can bid on items or just buyout, when the auction time is over highest bidder wins the item and the payment is handled by stripe.js

Search bar allows users to look for spesific item

Users can also view the items they added for auction and see the current highest bid details

Items are deleted after 7 days if nobody choses to bid on them
